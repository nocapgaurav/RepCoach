Exercise Form Tips

Bicep Curl

Keep your upper arm stationary. Full range of motion: 30° to 160°.

Squat

Keep your back straight and knees aligned with toes. Range: 90° to 160°.

Lateral Raise

Lift arms to shoulder height. Keep motion controlled.
